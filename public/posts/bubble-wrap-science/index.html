The Science of Bubble Wrap

published
November 27, 2024
reading time
1 minute

Bubble wrap wasn’t originally designed for packaging—it was invented in 1957 as a textured wallpaper! Today, it’s used to protect fragile items during shipping. The bubbles act as tiny air cushions, absorbing shocks and reducing impacts.

Fun Trivia: Popping bubble wrap is a universal stress reliever. Studies show the satisfying “pop” triggers dopamine release in the brain!
